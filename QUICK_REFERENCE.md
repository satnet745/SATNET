## What is SATNET GLOBAL in 30 Seconds?

A **single integrated app** combining everything you need:

### SATNET MESH (Communication Layer)
- Voice calls (direct P2P or multi-hop)
- Video calls (over mesh)
- Text messaging (no internet needed)
- File sharing
- Works offline with local devices

### BITCOIN (Currency Layer)
- Non-custodial wallet (you hold private keys)
- 12-word recovery phrase
- Send on-chain or via Lightning
- Self-custody only (SATNET never touches your funds)

### VOUCHER SYSTEM (Access Layer)
- Agents convert cash to Bitcoin
- Users scan QR → get Bitcoin
- Lightning settles instantly
- No intermediary holds funds

### REPUTATION (Trust Layer)
- Agents stake Bitcoin as collateral
- Community scores by reliability
- Transparent dispute resolution
- Automatic tier progression

### CENSORSHIP RESISTANCE (Freedom Layer)
- Works offline with mesh network
- No central server (can't be shut down)
- No account required (wallet-based identity)
- Works when internet is blocked

### For Users
1. Download app

4. Scan agent's voucher QR

### For Agents
1. Download app
2. Create wallet
3. Register as Agent
4. Stake Bitcoin (auto-promoted through tiers)
5. Generate vouchers
6. Share QR codes

### For Merchants
1. Download app
2. Create wallet
3. Register as Merchant

## ❓ FAQs

**Q: Is my Bitcoin safe?**  
A: Yes. You hold private keys locally. SATNET never accesses your Bitcoin.

**Q: What if the app disappears?**  
A: Your 12-word recovery phrase works with any Bitcoin wallet (Electrum, BlueWallet, etc.).

**Q: How do agents make money?**  
A: Voucher spread (4% margin). At 1,000 vouchers/day = $40/day profit.

**Q: What if an agent scams me?**  
A: Their staked Bitcoin is slashed. Community verifiers mediate.

**Q: Do I need internet?**  
A: No. Mesh network works offline. For blockchain sync, needs internet occasionally.

**Q: Is it legal?**  
A: Software is legal (free speech). Agent compliance varies by country.
