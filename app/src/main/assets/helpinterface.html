Using the Main Screen

The main screen is split into two cards. The first card focuses on communication across the mesh. The second card opens SATNET financial tools. Status text near the top shows whether the mesh is ready and whether SATNET startup has finished warming up.
The footer at the bottom shows your current SATNET phone number or identifier. You can still change your display name and number through the normal setup/account flows.

Main screen actions

CallMake calls via SATNET.
MessagesSend and receive MeshMS conversations.
ContactsBrowse peers and manage Android contacts linked to SATNET identities.
MapsOpen SATNET Maps for local-first mapping, encrypted bookmarks, and Rhizome bookmark exchange.
Share FilesOpen Rhizome to publish, browse, and save files shared on the mesh.
Share SATNETHelp another person install the app over local sharing methods without depending on an app store.
SettingsOpen account, logs, MeshMS, and legacy mesh-testing settings.
ConnectEnable SATNET services and control Wi-Fi, hotspot, Bluetooth, and legacy ad hoc networking.
HelpOpen the built-in guides, privacy notes, release notes, and support information.
SATNETOpen the SATNET role and Bitcoin tool flow. From there you can create or unlock a wallet, redeem vouchers, issue vouchers, access verifier tools, and use Lightning merchant tools where enabled.
The SATNET financial card becomes more useful after startup completes. Depending on your registered role and build flags, you may see wallet-only flows, agent voucher tools, voucher redemption, verifier review, and Lightning merchant tools.
