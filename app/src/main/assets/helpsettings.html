SATNET Settings

The modern SATNET settings screen is smaller than older versions. In current builds it focuses on account details, logs, MeshMS settings, and legacy mesh testing.
The old in-app Wi-Fi settings row still exists in the layout for compatibility, but it is hidden on modern Android because network configuration is handled from the Connect screen and by Android system settings.

Accounts ManagementReview your SATNET account identity, name, number, and related Android account information. More detail is available in Contacts, identity and SATNET roles.
View LogsDeveloper and troubleshooting view for local logs, useful when diagnosing network, startup, or device issues.
Mesh TestingLegacy compatibility test for ad hoc networking on rooted devices. This is risky, device-specific, and not the recommended path for most modern devices. See Ad Hoc Wi-Fi and legacy mesh testing.
MeshMS SettingsManage notification and messaging-related preferences for MeshMS conversations.
Connect ScreenNot opened from this list, but still important: day-to-day Wi-Fi, hotspot, and Bluetooth controls are handled from Connect.
SATNET Bitcoin wallet, voucher, merchant, verifier, and maps tools are not configured from this screen. Those are opened from the SATNET and Maps entries on the main screen.
