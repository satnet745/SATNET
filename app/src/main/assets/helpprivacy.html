Privacy summary

SATNET is designed to keep identity, communication, wallet, and mapping data primarily on-device unless you explicitly share or export it.
Calls, messages, peer discovery, Rhizome content, and optional SATNET provider requests can still move data across local networks, mesh peers, or configured external services depending on the feature you use.
Wallet seeds and recovery material should be treated as highly sensitive local secrets. Do not store them casually in screenshots, cloud notes, or unsafe backups.
SATNET Maps keeps temporary locations ephemeral until you save them, and saved bookmarks are encrypted on-device before any optional Rhizome sharing.
For the full policy text, open PRIVACY.md when internet access is available.
